You can send a **short, polite, professional email** asking them to verify and resend the referral invitation. Here are two good options.

---

### Option 1 – Simple & Professional

Hi Hardik,

Thank you for referring my friend for the position.

He has not yet received the automated email to apply and upload his resume. Could you please verify whether the referral invitation was sent successfully?

The email IDs that may have been used are:

* [azad05@msn.com](mailto:azad05@msn.com)
* [azad0525@gmail.com](mailto:azad0525@gmail.com)

If possible, could you please confirm which email ID the invitation was sent to or resend the application link?

Thank you for your help.

Best regards,
Eswar

---

### Option 2 – Slightly More Polite (Recommended)

Hi Hardik,

Thank you for referring my friend for the position.

He has not yet received the automated email to apply and upload his resume. Could you please check if the referral invitation was triggered successfully?

The possible email IDs are:

* [azad05@msn.com](mailto:azad05@msn.com)
* [azad0525@gmail.com](mailto:azad0525@gmail.com)

If convenient, could you please confirm the email ID used for the referral or resend the application invitation?

Appreciate your help.

Best regards,
Eswar

---

💡 **Tip:**
Also ask your friend to check:

* **Spam / Junk folder**
* **Promotions tab (Gmail)**

Sometimes referral emails from **Aetna/CVS systems** go there.
