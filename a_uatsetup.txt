During the QA portal URL alignment, we identified an authentication configuration dependency between the portal environment settings and the Ping client registration.

Historically, the flow was functioning based on an earlier client mapping, and when the QA-specific client configuration was introduced, the corresponding redirect URI registration needed to be aligned on the identity side as well.

This dependency became visible only during the environment transition, which resulted in the redirect URI validation issue. Once the URI mapping was updated and synchronized for the QA client, the login flow started working as expected
